AWS Elastic Disaster Recovery (DRS)

AWS Elastic Disaster Recovery (DRS) plays a crucial role in ensuring business continuity by offering organizations a resilient framework for recovering critical workloads and data in the event of unexpected disruptions. As companies increasingly rely on cloud-based digital infrastructure, AWS DRS provides a sophisticated, scalable, and automated approach to disaster recovery (DR) within the AWS cloud, ensuring operational resilience across various scenarios.
